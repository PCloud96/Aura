Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Aura: Face Analysis ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Your analysis results, preferences, and photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Camera and Photos

The App requests access to your device camera to capture photos for face analysis, and access to your photo library to select existing photos or save analysis results. All photo processing and face analysis is performed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No photos, facial data, or biometric information is ever uploaded to any server. Photos used for analysis are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by removing the app.

On-Device Face Analysis and Biometric Data

The App uses Apple's Vision framework to analyze facial features including face shape, color season, eye shape, lip shape, and symmetry. This analysis involves detecting facial landmark points and extracting geometric measurements from your face. Under applicable privacy laws, this data may be classified as biometric information.

All face analysis is performed entirely on your device. No facial data, measurements, biometric information, or analysis results are ever transmitted to any external server. Face analysis data is processed in memory and is not permanently stored on your device. The App does not perform facial recognition or biometric identification.

By using the face analysis features of this App, you consent to the on-device processing of your facial geometry for entertainment purposes only. Results are not intended as medical, professional, or scientific assessments.

Analytics

The App uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to collect anonymous usage signals such as which screens are viewed and which features are used. These signals contain no personal information, no device identifiers, and cannot be used to identify individual users. For more information, see TelemetryDeck's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases, including a weekly subscription and a lifetime access option, processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase receipts from Apple but does not receive any personal information. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you to use the App. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including analysis results, scan history, photos, preferences, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 17 and older. The App uses biometric facial analysis technology regulated under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and access is restricted to users aged 17 and older through an age gate. We do not knowingly collect personal information or biometric data from children under 17. If you believe a minor has used this App, please contact us and we will promptly address the situation.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

TelemetryDeck — for anonymous, privacy-focused usage analytics
RevenueCat — for subscription management
Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
